Lead Radar — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 21 June 2026 · App: Lead Radar (com.vayuras.leadradar) · Provider: Vayuras

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Lead Radar mobile application (“Lead Radar”, “the app”, “we”, “us”) collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. Lead Radar is a strictly business-to-business (B2B) directory and lightweight CRM for professionals. By using the app you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Phone number. When you sign in, we collect your mobile phone number so we can create your account and send you a one-time password (OTP). This is required to use the app.
  • Device information. We store a device identifier and a device label so we can enforce single-device licensing (one active device per license) and protect against abuse.
  • License and entitlement records. When you redeem a license key, we record which data pack(s) you have unlocked.
  • Support and report content. If you use in-app Feedback & support or the Report / request removal option, we receive the message, subject and related details you submit.

2. Information that stays on your device

Your private CRM activity — notes, lead statuses, follow-up schedules, custom categories, and any files you attach to a contact — is stored locally in the app’s database on your device. It is not uploaded to our servers. Imported and downloaded data packs are also stored on your device.

3. About the business listings

The B2B business listings shown in data packs are compiled from publicly available business sources and are provided for legitimate professional use (vendor discovery, business outreach, relationship management). They are business contact details, not consumer personal data. Every listing includes an in-app “Report / request removal” option. A business owner or data subject can flag a wrong number, an incorrect listing, or request removal directly from the app, and we review and action such requests.

4. How we use information

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure (including rate-limiting and brute-force protection).
  • To deliver and enforce the licenses and data packs you redeem.
  • To respond to your support messages and removal/correction reports.
  • To maintain, troubleshoot and improve the service.

5. How information is shared

  • Supabase — our backend, authentication and database hosting provider, acting as a data processor on our behalf.
  • SMS delivery provider — used to send your one-time login code, where configured.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use advertising networks, and we do not integrate third-party analytics or crash-reporting SDKs. We may disclose information if required by law.

6. Data retention and deletion

We retain your account information while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from More → Delete account inside the app. Deleting your account removes your authentication record and associated server-side data (entitlements, notes you submitted to us, follow-ups stored server-side, feedback) and releases your licenses. Data stored only on your device is removed when you delete the app or use the in-app removal options.

7. Security

All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Downloadable data packs are encrypted. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

8. Children

Lead Radar is a business tool intended for professional users aged 18 and over. It is not directed to children and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can exercise account deletion in-app, and you can contact us for other requests.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Effective date” above. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact us

For privacy questions or data requests, contact: dineshkumar.niet@gmail.com. You can also submit a report from within the app via Report / request removal or Feedback & support.
