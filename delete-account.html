Delete your Lead Radar account and data

App: Lead Radar (com.vayuras.leadradar) · Provider: Ahzimat

You can permanently delete your Lead Radar account and the data associated with it at any time, using either method below.

Option 1 — In the app (recommended)

  1. Open Lead Radar and sign in.
  2. Go to the More tab.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm in the dialog. Your account is deleted immediately and you are signed out.

Option 2 — By request (if you no longer have the app installed)

Email dineshkumar.niet@gmail.com from the email linked to your account, or include the mobile number you signed in with, and ask us to delete your account. We verify ownership of the number and action the request.

What is deleted

  • Your authentication record (the phone number you signed in with).
  • Your licenses / entitlements (redeemed data packs are released).
  • Any feedback, support messages and removal reports you submitted to us.

What is kept, and for how long

  • Data stored only on your device (notes, follow-ups, statuses, downloaded packs, file attachments) is removed when you uninstall the app or use the in-app removal options. It is never on our servers.
  • Server-side account data is deleted promptly on request; residual copies in encrypted backups are purged within 30 days.
  • We may retain minimal records only where required by law.
